About us

A focused team for a focused mission.

forBET Digital is the technology and media division of a long-established group. Our role is simple: build modern mobile products for sports audiences — fast, reliable, and beautifully made.

How we became a separate unit

For years, our parent organization operated as a single multi-disciplinary company with engineering as one of many internal functions. As the demand for high-quality mobile products grew — and as the technology landscape moved away from generalist teams toward specialized product squads — it became clear that digital craftsmanship deserved its own room to breathe.

In response, leadership made a deliberate decision: spin out the engineering, design and product talent into a dedicated digital unit with its own roadmap, culture and hiring autonomy. That unit is forBET Digital.

The separation gave us the freedom to set our own engineering standards, pick the tools we actually want to use, and partner with external clients on our own terms. It also let us concentrate, without compromise, on what we are good at: sports media and consumer mobile.

Our mission

"Deliver emotion through digital."

Sport is, fundamentally, an emotional product. A goal in the 90th minute, a saved penalty, a comeback that no model predicted — these moments are what people remember. The role of software, in our view, is not to compete with that feeling but to amplify it: get the right notification to the right person at the right second, present statistics in a way that adds context rather than noise, and design interfaces that fade into the background so the story can come through.

What we focus on

  • Consumer mobile applications built natively for iOS and Android, or cross-platform with Flutter where it makes sense.
  • Sports data and analytics products that translate raw event streams into clear, useful insights.
  • Media aggregation platforms that pull together news, highlights and editorial coverage from trusted sources.
  • Fan engagement tools — fixtures, alerts, polls and community features that bring matchday closer.

How we work

We are a small, senior team. We prefer depth over headcount, and we put a real value on the slow craft of building something well: writing readable code, designing screens that hold up over months of use, and shipping releases that do not embarrass us when we revisit them a year later.

Our process is intentionally lightweight — short cycles, real conversations, and clear ownership. Decisions are made by the people closest to the work, and reviewed openly. We borrow what makes sense from agile, scrum and shape-up, but we do not worship any of them.

Where we are based

The legal entity is registered in Warsaw, Poland. Day-to-day, the team is distributed — we hire wherever the right people are, and we structure work to support that. Our office in Warsaw functions as a coordination hub rather than a mandatory destination.

What we are not

We are a technology and media company. We do not build, operate or promote gambling products, betting platforms or casino software. Our work is focused entirely on sports media, analytics and fan-facing digital products, and the team you see here is the team you will work with on those products.
