News & updates

What we are working on, in our own words.

A short, honest record of announcements, releases and team milestones. We keep this page lean — no marketing posts, just things worth sharing.

February 2026

2026 roadmap: three product launches planned

We have locked in our roadmap for the year. Three product concepts — the media aggregator, the sports statistics app and the fan platform — will move from internal prototypes to closed beta during 2026, with public release windows to be confirmed quarter by quarter. We will share more concrete dates as we get closer to each launch, and we will be honest if anything slips.

January 2026

Engineering team expansion across Q1 and Q2

We are growing the engineering and design team this year to support the three product lines. Open roles include UI/UX Designer, Senior Flutter Developer, iOS and Android engineers, a Backend engineer and a Product Manager focused on sports media. We hire across the EU with a Warsaw home base, and we welcome both remote and hybrid candidates.

January 2026

forBET Digital established as a dedicated technology unit

We have completed the internal reorganization that establishes forBET Digital as a focused technology and media division. The unit now operates with its own engineering roadmap, design standards and hiring autonomy, and is concentrated entirely on mobile products for sports audiences. More about the rationale behind this move on our About page.

December 2025

Privacy and compliance review completed

Ahead of our first public app submissions, the team has completed an internal privacy and compliance review covering data handling, third-party SDK usage and platform store requirements. The published privacy policy on this site reflects the result of that review and will be kept current as our products evolve.
