Careers

The team is growing. Come help us build it.

We are a small, senior group that takes craft seriously. We hire slowly, give people real responsibility, and protect time for focused work. If that sounds like the environment you have been looking for, take a look at our open roles.

What it is like here

We work in small teams with short reporting lines. Engineers, designers and product people sit together — figuratively and, when possible, literally — and ship together. We avoid handoffs whenever we can.

We respect deep work. Meetings are bunched, defaults are async, and "no" is a perfectly reasonable answer to an unnecessary calendar invitation.

We pay competitively for the market we hire from, and we are open to remote, hybrid and on-site arrangements depending on the role.
